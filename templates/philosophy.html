tinytask is a daily task list for the things you have to do today.

Philosophy

Write down tasks.

Keep your mind clear and focused by offloading them to a list. Use your memory for more important things.

Keep it simple.

Your space is small, built for tiny tasks. If it can't be stated in a few words, your task may be too large. Break it into smaller tasks and spread them out.

Don't get bogged down.

Some tasks don't get finished. Some aren't important, though they seemed to be in the past. Important tasks will resurface in your life, and can be re-added or scheduled for a future date. You can move a task to tomorrow, but each list is specific to the date and unfinished business will remain in the past.

Mark a task as soon as you begin.

Tasks that you have started will rise to the top of the list. Use this to remind yourself of what you were just doing.

It's okay to put something you've already done on the list.

Keep a record of what you've accomplished. You might need it later.
